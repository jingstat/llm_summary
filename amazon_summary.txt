The company experienced an estimated employee severance charge of $470 million in the first quarter, including $270 million related to AWS. They made the decision to eliminate 9,000 roles in various departments. Despite this, their stores revenue and unit sales still grew more than their fulfillment expense and outbound shipping costs. Inflationary pressures eased quarter over quarter due to reductions in shipping rates, diesel fuel, and electricity costs. The company was able to make improvements in productivity in their fulfillment network and stabilize labor availability, allowing them to implement structural changes to improve delivery speed and cost efficiency.
